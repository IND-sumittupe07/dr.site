About The Doctor

A Surgeon Dedicated to
Restoring Your Mobility

12 years of surgical excellence, international training, and a patient-first philosophy.

Dr. Pramod Tupe is a highly accomplished Orthopedic and Spine Surgeon renowned for his expertise in minimally invasive spine surgery, complex joint replacements, and advanced arthroscopic procedures.

Trained at leading institutions across India and internationally, Dr. Tupe has pioneered several techniques in the region. His evidence-based, patient-centered approach ensures every individual receives a personalized treatment plan tailored to their unique condition and lifestyle goals.
